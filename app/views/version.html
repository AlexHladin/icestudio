Welcome to Icestudio!

This version of Icestudio, introduces very important changes in its kernel. Icestudio is starting to use multithreading for plugins and some tasks, improving overall performance and allowing you to do tasks really in parallel.

We need your support to detect new bugs and receive your feedback. Thanks a lot to try this WIP version.
